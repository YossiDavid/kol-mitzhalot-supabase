# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Shadchanim (Jewish matchmakers) managing shidduch proposals, and students (singles seeking matches) tracking their matchmaking process. Shadchanim work at a desk — typically web on desktop, occasionally mobile. Students check their status from any device.

## Product Purpose

A shidduch management system that replaces spreadsheets and paper-tracking for matchmakers. Shadchanim maintain a roster of candidates, drag-and-drop pairs onto a "shidduch desk" for compatibility review, and send/track proposals. Students see their own profile and incoming offers. The system handles the full lifecycle from candidate registration through proposal → acceptance/rejection.

## Brand Personality

Professional, modern, and efficient — the tool should feel like a polished B2B SaaS product that respects the gravity and sensitivity of the matchmaking process. Not a consumer app. Not a dating app.

## Anti-references

- Dating apps (Tinder, Hinge, Bumble): no swipe UX, no profile-card stacks, no gamified matching, no red/pink heart-heavy aesthetics
- Generic CRM (Salesforce-style): should not feel like a sterile enterprise admin panel

## Design Principles

1. **Clarity over cleverness** — every screen has one primary action. Labels are plain Hebrew. No jargon, no cutesy metaphors.
2. **Gravity and discretion** — this deals with personal, sensitive information. Design should feel respectful and composed, never loud or playful.
3. **Efficient workflows** — shadchanim handle many candidates. Dense-but-readable information layouts, keyboard-friendly, fast.
4. **Consistent hierarchy** — the sidebar (teal primary) anchors every screen. Content boxes use card whites on a cool light-gray background. Typography stays restrained.
5. **RTL-first** — the interface is Hebrew RTL. Every spacing, icon, and layout decision must be verified in RTL context.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Hebrew RTL throughout (`dir="rtl"`)
- WCAG 2.1 AA minimum contrast
- Reduced motion respected (no required animations)
- Keyboard navigation for all primary flows
